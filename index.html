About Us

Kytäjä is the perfect place for a successful and memorable event. You will certainly enjoy your stay in a beautiful and natural environment of the famous old Kytäjä Estate. High-quality rental villas with lake views are available even for larger groups.

Accommodation

Kytäjä Golf Stay&Play accommodation options offer hotel room-type accommodation in the middle of Kytäjä's most beautiful nature - right in the immediate vicinity of Kytäjä Golf's world-class golf courses.

Restaurant

From the Kytäjä Golf restaurant you can enjoy fantastic lake views in a relaxed atmosphere.

Driving Instructions

Kytäjä Golf is located in Hyvinkää, in the village of Kytäjä, 12 km from the Helsinki – Tampere freeway.
